Q&A About My IITB Journey

What was your first impression of IITB?

My first impression was overwhelming - the campus was huge, the buildings were impressive, and everyone seemed so smart and accomplished. It took me a while to adjust, but I quickly fell in love with the place.

What's your favorite thing about IITB?

The best thing about IITB is the people. The diversity of ideas, the brilliant minds, and the supportive community make it a truly special place to learn and grow.

What challenges did you face in your first year?

The biggest challenge was balancing academics with hostel life. Time management was tough, but I learned to prioritize and stay organized.
